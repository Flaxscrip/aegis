================================================================================
AEGIS / HEARTHOLD — FINANCIAL EVIDENCE, SELECTIVELY DISCLOSED
================================================================================
Prove a compliance fact without disclosing the data behind it. The Warden
assembles the customer's quarterly income records into a Merkle-rooted, signed
evidence graph and attests a DERIVED fact -- "annual income exceeds the
$200,000 accredited-investor threshold." A verifier (a fund / onboarding desk)
learns the fact and that records back it, but never the figures. Selective
disclosure lets an auditor spot-check ONE quarter against the signed root while
the rest stay hidden. Because the income records are sensitive, the disclosure
carries the Sovereign's second-factor (Signet) co-sign -- embedded and
independently verifiable, no decryption.

Runs entirely on the air-gapped node (see 01-isolation-proof.txt).
Command:  ./deploy/sandbox/run-finance.sh
================================================================================
