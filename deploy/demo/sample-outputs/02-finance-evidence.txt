================================================================================
AEGIS / HEARTHOLD — FINANCIAL EVIDENCE: ISSUER-ATTESTED, SELECTIVELY DISCLOSED
================================================================================
Prove a compliance fact without disclosing the data behind it -- and let the
data carry a real institution's signature, not the custodian's word.

Two parts, both on the air-gapped node (see 01-isolation-proof.txt):

  PART A  A 3rd-party BANK issues a signed balance statement (a verifiable
          credential) into the customer's PRIVATE vault. The custodian holds
          it but CANNOT read the figure at rest; the owner can; trust in the
          figure rests on the BANK's signature.

  PART B  The Warden assembles quarterly income records into a Merkle-rooted,
          signed evidence graph and attests a DERIVED fact -- "annual income
          exceeds the $200,000 accredited-investor threshold." A verifier
          learns the fact and that records back it, never the figures.
          Selective disclosure lets an auditor spot-check ONE quarter against
          the signed root; the rest stay hidden. A Sovereign (2nd-factor
          Signet) co-sign is embedded and independently verifiable.
================================================================================
